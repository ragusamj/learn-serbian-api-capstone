Welcome! This application is an excellent source for advanced Serbo-Croatian language and culture students.
This app has two features:
You can choose to translate text from English into Serbo-Croatian.
You can also find a book in Serbo-Croatian to practice your extensive reading.

Welcome to Serbo-Croatian translator! After you recieve the translation in Serbo-Croatian, you will be able to learn more about the words by clicking on google or google images icons.
The google search will provide you with relevant sources in Serbo-Croatian. You will be able to see how the translated portion works in a sentence, contextual topic, song lyrics and other cultural elements.
The google images act as a pictionary. You can click on it to see the meaning of the word in pictures.
